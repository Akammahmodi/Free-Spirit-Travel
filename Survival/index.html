Spirit Animal: Fearless Lion(ess)

The Fearless lion(ess) jumps in the unknown. He(She) takes the lead, stands up for his(her) believes and doesn't doubt his(her) choices. In this story you are a visionary that has been banned from society to live a life of exile. As you explore your new home, you realise that you are not alone. You discover an ancient tribe that has been living off the grid for many centuries. To join the tribe, you have to prove yourself worthy. You will have to complete a series of physical and mental trials. Are you up for the challenge?
