Welcome to Adventure

At Free-Spirit-travel nothing is impossible. While you explore the borders of your wildest imagination, we are here to guide you on every step of the way. The journey of your lifetime awaits you.

Spirit Animal: Spirited Monkey

The Spirited Monkey has a playful heart. He(She) explores his/her inner child and looks at the world in new and different ways.
You are a treasure hunter, looking for the lost treasure of Ibn Battuta. After an extensive search on Geosearch you manage to pinpoint the approximate whereabouts of the treasure and set sail to your destinaton. There you will meet the locals that might help you when you look for hidden clues and solve the mysteries.
Let's get cracking!

Spirit Animal: Fearless Lion(ess)

The Fearless lion(ess) jumps in the unknown. He(She) takes the lead, stands up for his(her) believes and doesn't doubt his(her) choices.
In this story you are a visionary that has been banned from society to live a life of exile. As you explore your new home, you realise that you are not alone. You discover an ancient tribe that has been living off the grid for many centuries. To join the tribe, you have to prove yourself worthy. You will have to complete a series of physical and mental trials.
Are you up for the challenge?
